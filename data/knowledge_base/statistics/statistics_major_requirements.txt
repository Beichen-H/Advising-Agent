STATISTICS MAJOR REQUIREMENTS (UIUC)
=====================================
Department of Statistics, College of Liberal Arts & Sciences
Contact: stat-undergrad@illinois.edu | (217) 333-2167 | 156 Computing Applications Building

The Statistics major is designed to provide students with an understanding of the concepts of 
statistical inference and a familiarity with the methods of applied statistical analysis. 
A major in statistics will prepare students for a career in business, industry, or government, 
and for further graduate study in statistics or in a related area.

REQUIRED COURSES:
-----------------
Total credit hours in major: 42-45 hours
LAS general education requirements also apply
Total needed for graduation: 120 hours

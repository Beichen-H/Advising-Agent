MINOR IN SOCIOLOGY (UIUC)
=========================
Total Hours: Minimum 18 hours of SOC credit

COURSE REQUIREMENTS:
-------------------
1. SOC 100: Introduction to Sociology (4 hrs)
   Description: An overview of the topics of sociology and of various sociological 
   perspectives, and of how scholars in the discipline study social phenomena.
   Note: This course is a prerequisite for most two-hundred level courses.
   *If using transfer credit, some schools grant only 3 credits for SOC 100.*

2. SOC 280: Introduction to Social Statistics (4 hrs)
   Description: An introduction to the statistical measures and techniques used in the 
   social sciences. This course is a prerequisite for Sociology 380.
   
   *Other courses that meet this requirement (but do NOT count toward the 18 hours of 
   Sociology courses):*
   - STAT 100
   - PSYCH 235
   - ECON 202
   - MATH 161
   - UP 161
   - Parkland College: MATH 108 and MATH 160
   
   Important: If a statistics course is taken outside of Sociology to fulfill this 
   requirement, that course does NOT count toward the 18 hours of Sociology courses.

3. SOC 200: Introduction to Sociological Theory (3 hrs)
   Description: This course is an introduction to the foundations of sociological theory. 
   Topics may include the problem of social order and the nature of social conflict; 
   capitalism and bureaucracy; the relationship between social structure and politics; 
   and the evolution of modern societies.
   Prerequisite: Sophomore standing

4. Advanced Level Sociology Courses (6 hrs)
   Select at least TWO 300- or 400-level Sociology courses
   (Refer to Course Catalog for available courses)

5. Elective Sociology Hours (3-6 hrs)
   Select TWO to THREE 100-, 200-, 300-, or 400-level Sociology courses to complete 
   the 18 credit hour minimum requirement.
   (Refer to Course Catalog for available courses)

SUMMARY:
--------
Required courses: SOC 100 + SOC 280 + SOC 200 = 11 hours
Advanced courses: 6 hours (two 300/400-level courses)
Electives: 3-6 hours (to reach 18 total)
Total: Minimum 18 hours of SOC credit

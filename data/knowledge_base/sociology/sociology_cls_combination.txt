SOCIOLOGY MAJOR + CRIMINOLOGY, LAW, AND SOCIETY MINOR COMBINATION
==================================================================
Total Hours: 41 hours

CORE CLASSES (7 courses total):
------------------------------
1. SOC 100 - Introduction to Sociology (4 hours)
   *Note: If using transfer credit, some schools only grant 3 hours*

2. SOC 200 - Introduction to Sociological Theory (3 hours)

3. SOC 275 - Criminology (3 hours)

4. SOC 280 - Introduction to Social Statistics (4 hours)
   *Statistics requirement can be fulfilled through:*
   - STAT 100
   - PSYC 235
   - ECON 202
   - MATH 161
   - Parkland College: MATH 108, MATH 160
   *⚠️ These alternatives do NOT count towards overall SOC hours*

5. SOC 380 - Social Science Research Methods (4 hours)

6. SOC 479 - Law and Society (3 hours)

7. SOC Capstone - Choose ONE (3 hours)
   - SOC 400: Internships
   - SOC 450: Senior Capstone Seminar
   - SOC 495: Senior Honors Seminar (requires 3.5 GPA and instructor consent)

ELECTIVES:
---------
After completing core classes, select additional courses to reach 41 total hours.
- At least 9 hours must be at the 300/400 level
- Available elective topics include:
  * Sociology of Gender
  * Introduction to Poverty
  * Issues in Sociology
  * Career Development
  * Sexualities
  * Race & Ethnicity
  * Criminology
  * Health, Illness & Society
  * Sociology of Deviance
  * Technology & Society
  * Law & the Commons
  * Social Movements
  * And more!

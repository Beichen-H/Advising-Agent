SOCIOLOGY PROGRAMS OVERVIEW - UIUC DEPARTMENT OF SOCIOLOGY
===========================================================

1. SOCIOLOGY MAJOR (32 hours)
   - Required for students who want to major in Sociology
   - Full degree program with comprehensive requirements
   - See: sociology_major_requirements.txt for details

2. SOCIOLOGY MINOR (18 hours)
   - For students who want to complement their major with Sociology
   - Required courses: SOC 100, SOC 280, SOC 200
   - Two advanced courses (300/400 level)
   - Electives to reach 18 hours
   - See: sociology_minor_requirements.txt for details

3. CRIMINOLOGY, LAW & SOCIETY (CLS) MINOR (18 hours)
   - For students interested in law, justice, or advocacy careers
   - Required: SOC 100/163, SOC 275, SOC 479
   - Three sociology electives
   - See: cls_minor_requirements.txt for details

4. SOCIOLOGY + CLS COMBINATION (41 hours)
   - Combines Sociology major with CLS minor
   - Reduced total hours compared to separate programs
   - 7 core classes, then electives to reach 41 hours
   - See: sociology_cls_combination.txt for details

IMPORTANT NOTES FOR ALL PROGRAMS:
- Statistics alternatives (STAT 100, etc.) fulfill requirements but do NOT count as SOC hours
- Transfer credit for SOC 100 may be only 3 hours
- Supporting coursework (for major) cannot include SOC or cross-listed courses

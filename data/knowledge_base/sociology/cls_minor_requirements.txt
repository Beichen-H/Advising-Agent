MINOR IN CRIMINOLOGY, LAW, & SOCIETY (CLS) - UIUC
=================================================
Total Hours: 18 credit hours (6 courses)

Thinking about a career in law, justice, or advocacy? The criminology, law, and society 
minor is for you. In the CLS minor, you'll take a range of courses that provide theoretical 
and practical knowledge while exposing you to current sociological, legal, and justice issues.

COURSE REQUIREMENTS:
-------------------
1. Introductory Course (3-4 hours) - Choose ONE:
   - SOC 100: Introduction to Sociology
   - SOC 163: Social Problems

2. SOC 275: Criminology (3 hours)

3. SOC 479: Law and Society (3 hours)

4. Three Sociology Electives (9 hours)
   Choose from courses such as:
   - Sociology of Gender
   - Race and Ethnicity
   - Sociology of Deviance
   - Social Inequality
   - And more!

TOTAL: 18 credit hours

This minor is an easy addition to any of your studies at the University of Illinois!
